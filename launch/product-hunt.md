**Schedule**: Day 2-3 after HN launch (ride the wave)

## Listing Details

**Name**: Atlas
**Tagline**: Portfolio intelligence for your repos
**Description**:

Atlas scans all your repositories and gives you a health dashboard in 30 seconds. Every AI coding tool works inside a single repo -- Atlas sees the full picture across your entire portfolio.

Health scoring across 4 dimensions (tests, git hygiene, documentation, structure). Tech stack detection for 10+ languages and 15+ frameworks. Cross-project intelligence finds shared dependencies, version mismatches, and health gaps.

Zero network calls. Zero telemetry. Everything runs locally. 100% free and open source (MIT).

**Topics**: Developer Tools, Open Source, CLI, DevOps, Python
**Pricing**: Free
**Platforms**: macOS, Linux, Windows (Python 3.11+)

## First Comment (Maker)

Hey Product Hunt! I'm Asif, and I built Atlas because managing 10+ repos with no cross-project visibility was driving me crazy.

The insight: every tool on the market works inside one repo. Cursor, Copilot, Claude Code -- they're all single-repo. But the questions that matter most ("which repos have zero tests?", "are my React versions consistent?") require seeing across repos.

Atlas runs locally in your terminal. No signup, no cloud, no telemetry. `pip install nxtg-atlas` and you're scanning in 30 seconds.

I'd love your feedback on what cross-project signals you'd find most useful!
